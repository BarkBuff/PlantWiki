Standard Operating Procedure: Dryer Unit Restart

1. Ensure the dryer is powered off.
2. Wait 3 minutes for internal cooling.
3. Press and hold RESET for 5 seconds.
4. Power ON the main switch.
5. Confirm green status light before operation.

Common Issues:
- Red light blinking: Check thermal overload.
- No startup: Ensure power breaker is ON.
